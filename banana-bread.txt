Grandma's Banana Bread

1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
2 cups mashed ripe bananas
2 eggs
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt

Cream butter and sugar together then add the eggs.
Mix dry ingredients in separate bowl.
Add mashed bananas to the butter and sugar.
Mix in the dry ingredients, mixing only until combined.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour.
